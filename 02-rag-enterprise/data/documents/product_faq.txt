Product FAQ — Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Go to Settings > Security > Change Password. You'll need to enter your current password and then your new password twice. If you've forgotten your current password, click "Forgot Password" on the login page to receive a reset link via email.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and bank transfers. For enterprise plans, we also support invoicing with NET-30 terms. Contact billing@company.com for custom payment arrangements.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: You can cancel at any time from Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription. Your access will continue until the end of the current billing period. Refunds are available within 14 days of purchase for annual plans.

Q: What is the uptime SLA?
A: We guarantee 99.9% uptime for all paid plans. This excludes scheduled maintenance windows (Sundays 2:00-4:00 AM UTC). Status page: status.company.com. Incident history and post-mortems are publicly available.

Q: How do I contact support?
A: Email: support@company.com (response within 4 hours for paid plans). Live chat: Available Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM UTC. Enterprise plans include a dedicated account manager and priority phone support.

Q: Is my data secure?
A: All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We are SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. Data is stored in AWS EU (Frankfurt) region. We conduct annual penetration testing by an independent security firm.

Q: Can I export my data?
A: Yes, you can export all your data at any time from Settings > Data > Export. Exports are available in CSV, JSON, and Parquet formats. Enterprise plans support automated scheduled exports via API.

Q: What integrations are available?
A: We integrate with Slack, Jira, GitHub, GitLab, PagerDuty, Datadog, and Zapier. Custom integrations are available via our REST API and webhooks. Enterprise plans include dedicated integration support.
