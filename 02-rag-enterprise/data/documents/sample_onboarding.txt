Employee Onboarding Guide

Welcome to the team! This guide will help you get started.

Week 1: Getting Started
- Day 1: Meet your manager and team members. Complete HR paperwork and set up your company accounts.
- Day 2: IT setup - receive your laptop, configure email, Slack, and development tools.
- Day 3: Review company handbook and complete mandatory compliance training.
- Day 4: Shadow a team member to understand daily workflows.
- Day 5: First one-on-one with your manager to discuss goals and expectations.

Week 2: Deep Dive
- Attend architecture overview sessions with the engineering team.
- Set up your development environment following the README in our main repository.
- Complete security awareness training and obtain VPN access.
- Join your first sprint planning meeting.

Week 3-4: Ramp Up
- Pick up your first ticket from the backlog (labeled "good-first-issue").
- Submit your first pull request and get familiar with our code review process.
- Attend product demo and provide feedback.
- Schedule meetings with cross-functional team members (design, QA, product).
